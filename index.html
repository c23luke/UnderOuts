⭐ PLAY score — the actual best-bet ranking

Only appears when a live line is loaded. It blends three things: the Under Lean (is there a real reason he goes short), the cushion (how far his projected outs sit below the line — more room = safer), and the price (plus money helps, heavy juice like −175 hurts because the market already knows).
⭐ Best plays ranks every pitcher with a line by edge — best on top, no cutoff. A low Under Lean can still rank high if the line sits well above his projection (big + cushion). The bigger the cushion, the safer the under.
